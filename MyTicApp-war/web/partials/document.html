Developers today increasingly recognize the need for distributed, transactional, and
portable applications that leverage the speed, security, and reliability of server-side
technology. Enterprise applications (EA) provide the business logic for an enterprise.
They are centrally managed and often interact with other enterprise software.
In the world of IT, EA must be designed, built, and
produced for less money, with greater speed, and with fewer resources.
MyTicApp provides the integration platform for the front-end applications as well as the back-end applications.
The emphasis is on "EA" frameworks.
Using the JavaEE model.
